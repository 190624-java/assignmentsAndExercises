WaterFall



It's a cascade SDLC model, where developement process moves step by step through each phase. Not a reversible process.


  • Requirements: All requirements are gathered into a requirements document.
  • Design: System design is created based on requirements. Includs specifications of hardwarew and software.
  • Implementation: Do eet! System is built in chunks according to specs.
  • Verification: Each chunk of implementation is tested.
  • Maintenance: Product is deployed and updated as needed to fix any further bugs.
